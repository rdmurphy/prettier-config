## [2.0.0] - 2021-09-23

### Added

- Added `bracketSameLine`, `embeddedLanguageFormatting` and `vueIndentScriptAndStyle`.
- Added [`prettier-plugin-svelte`](https://github.com/sveltejs/prettier-plugin-svelte) specific options.
